About Us:

CustomerCrowned was started when the founder had an everyday, simple, interaction with a nationwide and internationally-known tools manufacturing company. When he called their headquarters regarding a tool whose batteries where both not charging, thus making the tool practically inoperable and useless. He was told he needed to send in the complete unit to them, with the receipt, so that they can inverstigate whose fault it it was. He thought this was just crazy to provide or offer an unsatisfactory response. Being enterpreneurial himself and obseesed with customer servie and customer relations, he decided to start an online business that makes the custmers the king and queen in any compqny willing to listen to their customers and thrive in the process. >As an online platform we, therefore, provide a medium where customers discuss their opinions and or issues with the companies they do business or interact with. We invite the companies to participate in this interactive outreach by monitoring and responding to the customers' reactions, feedback and issues. We belleive no customer issues, opinions or feeback are too trivial or too hard to address by the businesses.

Those customer-oriented companies who want to go above and beyond just the reviews and very much hyped Customer relations mangagements (CRM) systems, are invited to search for customer relations excellence by taking this interactive and personalized approach on this platform.

Our Goal:

Our mission is to bring the the customers and businesses closer to each other in a personal and mutually fulfilling way. This allows and enables the businesses to isten to the customers' needs and their feedback so that they can not only take immediate action, but also chart the roadmap for a successful, user-freindly experience.
